QR Prism — Brand Logos Folder
==============================
Place SVG or PNG brand logo files here.
The filename (without extension) becomes the logo ID.

Examples:
  facebook.svg  → id: 'facebook'
  youtube.png   → id: 'youtube'

Recommended IDs:
  facebook, instagram, youtube, twitter, github, linkedin
  whatsapp, telegram, tiktok, spotify, discord, snapchat
  reddit, pinterest, paypal, twitch, apple, google
  bitcoin, ethereum, litecoin, dogecoin, solana

After adding files, they will auto-appear in the Logo grid.
