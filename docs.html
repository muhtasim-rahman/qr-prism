QR Types

QR Prism supports 16 data types. Each type has a purpose-built form.

URL / Website

Enter any web address. If you omit https:// it is added automatically.

https://example.com

Social Media

Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, YouTube, WhatsApp, Telegram — just enter your username and the correct URL is built for you automatically. The matching brand logo is also applied to the QR center.

WiFi

Generates a WiFi QR that phones can scan to join a network instantly.

WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;H:false;;
Use Error Correction H when adding logos. Scanning a WiFi QR requires the app to support it — iOS Camera and most Android QR apps do.

vCard Contact

Creates a scannable digital business card (vCard 3.0). Fields: Name, Phone, Email, Company, Title, Website, Address.

Design Options

Colors & Gradients

The Colors accordion has three tabs:

  • Background — Solid color, linear/radial gradient, or fully transparent
  • Foreground — Module color with optional gradient overlay
  • Eye Colors — Custom colors for the 3 finder squares (frame + inner dot), with per-eye gradient support
When Eye Colors is enabled, the gap between the eye frame and inner dot becomes transparent — giving a premium floating effect.

Patterns & Eyes

Three sub-tabs: Dot Design (22 patterns), Eye Frame (14 shapes), Eye Inner (14 shapes).

Additional sliders: Module Gap adds breathing room between modules (0–20%), and Eye Scale adjusts the size of the finder squares (70–130%).

Logo Embedding

Upload a custom logo (JPG/PNG/SVG, max 5 MB) or pick from 20 brand presets including WhatsApp, Instagram, GitHub, Bitcoin and more.

Always use Error Correction H when embedding a logo. Keep logo size under 30% of QR area for reliable scanning.

Frames

Choose from 12 frame styles — with or without a label. Frames with labels include Bottom Bar, Top Bar, Rounded Box, Badge, and Polaroid. Frameless options include Thin Border, Corner Brackets, Circle, and Hexagon.

The Frame Padding slider controls spacing between the QR code and the frame border.

Advanced

Rotation (0°/90°/180°/270°), flip horizontal/vertical, CSS filters (grayscale, sepia, hue-shift), color inversion, and shadow with blur/offset controls.

The QR Analytics panel shows character count, QR version, module grid, and a Scanability Score (0–100) with level breakdown.

Projects

Projects store the full state of a QR code: its data, type, design settings, and a PNG thumbnail.

Auto-save runs 1 second after you stop editing (if enabled in Settings). Manual saving via Save Project lets you name it. Projects are listed in three tabs: Saved, Auto-saved, and Pinned.

Each project card has actions: Load in Editor, Pin, Rename, Download PNG, and Delete. You can also search by name, data content, or tag, and multi-select for bulk delete or download.

Projects can be exported to JSON and re-imported on any device. If signed in, they sync to your Firebase account.

QR Scanner

Scan QR codes using your device camera or from an image file.

  • Tap Start Scanner and allow camera permission
  • Point at a QR code — it detects automatically
  • Or tap the Gallery icon to scan from an image file
  • Use the Flashlight button in low-light conditions
  • After scanning: Open URL, Copy, Edit in Generator, or Scan Again

Account & Cloud Sync

Sign in with email/password or Google to unlock cloud sync. Your projects, templates, and settings are stored in Firebase Realtime Database and sync across all your devices.

Signed-out users can still use all features — data is stored locally in localStorage. Signing in merges local data with the cloud.

Bug report images are uploaded to ImgBB (our image host), and only the URLs are stored in Firebase — no binary data in the database.

PWA / Offline Support

QR Prism is a Progressive Web App. Install it from the browser for:

  • Offline access — works without internet after first load
  • App-like experience — no browser chrome, full-screen
  • Home screen shortcut — launch like a native app
  • Faster load times — assets served from cache

To install: look for the Install banner in Settings, or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" / "Install App" option.

Scanability Tips

High contrast

Dark modules on a light background scan most reliably. Avoid light-on-light or very similar colors.

Minimum size

Print at least 2 cm × 2 cm (200px at 96 dpi). Larger is always better.

Error Correction H

Always use H when adding logos or heavy patterns. It allows up to 30% of the QR to be obscured and still scan.

Logo under 30%

Keep logo size at 25% or less of the QR area. Larger logos reduce scanability significantly.

Quiet zone

Keep the quiet zone (white margin) at 4 modules or more. This helps scanners locate the QR boundary.

Sharp output

Use PNG 4× or SVG for printing to ensure pixel-perfect, crisp edges.

FAQ

Is my data sent to any server?

No. All QR generation happens locally in your browser. If you're signed in, your projects and templates sync to Firebase. Bug report images go to ImgBB. Nothing else leaves your device.

Why isn't my QR code scanning?

See the Scanability Tips section above. Common causes: low contrast, logo too large, output too small, or low error correction level.

Can I use QR Prism offline?

Yes — after your first visit, the app is cached by the Service Worker and works fully offline. Install it as a PWA for the best offline experience.

What formats can I download?

PNG, PNG 2×, PNG 4×, SVG, WebP, JPG, and PDF. Enable Transparent BG in the dropdown for transparent exports.

How do I report a bug?

Use the Report Bug section in the app, or email qrprism@gmail.com.

Is QR Prism open source?

Yes! The source code is available on GitHub under the MIT License.
