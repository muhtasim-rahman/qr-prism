QR Not Scanning?

🔍 Low contrast — Use dark foreground on light background.
📐 Too small — At least 200px / 2cm when printed.
🖼️ Logo too large — Keep logo under 30% of QR area.
⚙️ Error correction — Use H (Best) when adding a logo.
🖨️ Blurry print — Ensure sharp edges, no pixelation.
📷 Camera distance — Hold 15–30 cm from the code.
📶 WiFi QR format — WIFI:T:WPA;S:name;P:pass;;
