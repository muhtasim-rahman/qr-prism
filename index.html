QR Not Scanning?

Low contrast

Use dark foreground on a light background for best results.

Too small

Print at least 200px / 2cm. Larger is always safer.

Logo too large

Keep the logo under 30% of the QR area. Use Error Correction H.

Error correction

Use H (Best) when adding logos or decorative patterns.

Blurry print

Download SVG or PNG 4× for sharp, crisp printing.

Camera distance

Hold 15–30 cm from the code, keep steady.

WiFi QR format

Must use correct format: WIFI:T:WPA;S:name;P:pass;;
