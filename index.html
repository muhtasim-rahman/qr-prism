Why isn't my QR scanning?

🔍 Low contrast — Use dark foreground on light background.
📐 Too small — Make QR at least 200px / 2cm when printed.
🖼️ Logo too large — Keep logo under 30% of QR area.
⚙️ Error correction — Use H (Best) when adding a logo.
🖨️ Blurry print — Ensure sharp edges, no blur or pixelation.
📷 Camera distance — Hold camera 15–30 cm from the code.
